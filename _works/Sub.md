## Sub Luce Maligna
2026<br>
in collaboration with Abigail Jara

{% include gallery class="fourth" caption="Images of the performance of *Sub Luce Maligna* at Casa de la Paz theater, Mexico City." %}

*They walk through the shadows under the lonely night<br>
and through the empty houses of Dis and its ghostly realms<br>
like the path under a malevolent and mysterious light<br>
it leads into the woods with an uncertain moon<br>
when Jupiter hid the sky with shadow<br>
and the black night stole the color from things.*<br>
Virgil, "Aeneid", Book VI

Concept & artistic direction: *Abigail Jara & Juan Pampín*

Choreography and costume design: *Abigail Jara*

Composition, interactive sound & video: *Juan Pampin*

Lighting design: *Raúl Mendoza Rosas*

Technical assistance: *Daniel Peterson & Cristina Brambila*

Performers Plataforma UV: *Selene Sore, Braulio Escobedo, Lexi Barradas, Kun Betza, Rodrigo Caudillo*

Performers DAJU (UNAM): *Sof Jara, Diego Saldaña Ramírez, Areli Itzel Ávila Acosta, Fernando Daniel Ocampo Mata, Erick David Falcón Morales, Catherine García Jiménez*

Co-production: *DXARTS, University of Washington; Facultad de Danza, Universidad Veracruzana; Teatro Casa de la Paz, Universidad Autónoma Metropolitana*
