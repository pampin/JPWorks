## Time and Time Again
2024<br>
in collaboration with Leo Nuñez

{% include gallery class="fourth" caption="Images of the *Time and Time Again* exhibition at Fundación Andreani, Buenos Aires" %}

In *Time and Time Again*, various devices, digital technologies, artificial intelligence, and other mechanisms converge to highlight—with humor and irony—certain paradoxes of the Argentine economy and politics. Drawing upon elements from different eras—such as taximeters, clocks, bicycle wheels, and AI-generated banknotes—the exhibition presents a dizzyingly paced installation in which time, much like the trajectory of the economy, resolves into a state of circularity.
